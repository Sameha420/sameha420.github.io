Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Sameha Rahman

Last posted: 27-08-2024

Sameha's Profile

Prep time: 5-10 minutes

Cook time: 50-60 minutes

The BEST chocolate chip banana bread that's flavourful and beautifully moist.

Equipment

  • 9x5" Loaf pan
  • Mixing bowls
  • Hand or stand mixer
  • Baking paper

    • Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour (210g)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar (150g)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter room temperature (113g)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 very ripe bananas mashed (1 1/2 cups)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
    • If your bananas aren't ripe then you can bake or microwave them until brown. Dark chocolate chips can be used or milk chocolate chips or even both!

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350F (180C). Grease and line a 9x5-inch loaf pan with baking spray or butter along with baking paper.

    2. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together.

    3. In a large bowl or the bowl of a stand mixer, beat the sugar and butter together on medium-high speed until fluffy and pale in color for about 3 minutes.

    4. Mix in the eggs one at a time while stopping and scraping down the bowl, then beat in the vanilla extract. Mix in the mashed bananas.

    5. With the mixer on low, add the dry ingredients and mix together until almost combined.

    6. Reserve a handful of the chocolate chips and stir the rest into the batter. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, and sprinkle the reserved chocolate chips on top

    7. Bake for 60 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean when inserted into the middle. Let cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then remove and finish cooling.
